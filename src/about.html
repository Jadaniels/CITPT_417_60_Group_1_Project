History of the Club

The club started as a group of friends sharing books we love. It went on this way for several years. Eventually we decided to bring it online to attract new members to share our love of stories with. It was just slightly increased group of local book lovers until one day we decided to start reviewing and recommending books to a wider audience. We noticed that many of our members fall into the young adult category so have since geared the site more to that audience. Our numbers have swelled even more since.

  • Contact us through email and we'll be in touch!

Why Become a Member?

Membership is open to anyone who's interested. It is particularly valuable to educators and students. The monthly cost is $15 a month and comes with several benefits listed below.

  • Access to our archive of reviews and free stories.
  • One free book a month of your choice.
  • Access to regular emails that include information on new books, exclusive deals, lesson plans, and more!
  • Your own content page to post you own reviews and access to our forums.
  • And there's more! Fill our the form to receive more information and to create a login. From there you can purchase your membership!
