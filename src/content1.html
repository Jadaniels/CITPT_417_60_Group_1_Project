Historical Books for Young Adults

Welcome to history! If you're interested in historical stories, told through the eyes of young men and women, this is the place for you! Each week Jim finds two books to read, review, and recommend. Most of the books reviewed are set in WW2, but other parts of history are also explored.

About the Reviewer

Hi there! Thank you for checking out my content. I'm an aspiring web developer, that also has a strong interest in the social sciences. I'm also a lover of good stories; wether told through movies, books, video games, pomes, or are simply told around a camp fire. I have a particular interest in stories set in historical periods that are told through the experiences of individuals. This is what the content of this page is dedicated to. I hope these stories, told through the eyes of young people like yourself, inspire you as much as they inspire me.
