Joel's Corner

Greeting and Salutations,

Thank you for taking the time to stop by my corner of the world. My family consists of my wife, three kids, ages 21, 19, and 16, two silly dogs and two pretentious cats. We read to our kids early on and they carry it through to their adulthood. My oldest has a book collection that rivals mine and we often discuss plot points over coffee. My middle son is more into movies, but I have gotten him to read the book version of several stories to elaborate the film. The youngest has a huge alternative book collection that I am attempting to read.

I am an avid reader of all genre, but take a special interest in seeing a different view of standard stories. I joined Simple Reads in May 2020 while attending my Senior Year of Lewis-Clark State College. While majoring in Web Design and Development I was introduced to a group of students with an interest in collaborating to create a website to share our books with you. When not in class, I work full time for the military. While on training exercises and deployment, I spend a lot of time away from friends and family. I find comfort in reading books that allow me temporary breaks from the hardships of separation. I connect to those around me by sharing my reading material. I often write to family members of the stories I read. We sometime read the same book together and we bond over characters. Many times, we argue about what the characters should be doing. I hope that you will enjoy my currently read list and share with your friends and family.

"Fools have a habit of believing that everything written by a famous author is admirable. For my part I read only to please myself and like only what suits my taste."

‐Voltaire
